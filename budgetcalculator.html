Budget Calculator

At CART as a way to practice our Javascript skills, we worked for EECU to make a budget calculator. They wanted to have it in their website so users can use it at the same time that they are looking for a Credit Union.

I love my website, I really like the design and how the program works. We were told to use an API to get information of different salaries of carriers. That was my second time working with APIs. In this website I applied my Javascript skills, to make a dynamic website that gets the user to the process of analyzing his or her budget.
